Executive Summary: Charting the Future of Personalized Learning in Indonesia

Our Goals:

1. Increase student retention on the platform by 25% within the first year.

2. Upsell courses leading to a 15% revenue growth in the second quarter post-implementation.

3. Become the go-to platform for STEM and language education in Indonesia.

The Challenge:

The current EdTech market in Indonesia shows significant gaps in personalized learning, STEM education, and teacher support. Our competitors are offering limited subjects and exam preparation, making it crucial for CoLearn to innovate and diversify its offerings
Our Solution:

We're introducing five transformative ideas to re-imagine CoLearn's approach to education. These include the use of gamification for enhanced student engagement, AI tools for streamlined content creation, and personalized learning pathways
