The Five Big Ideas to  Re-Imagine Co-Learn

Idea 1: Gamification

Injecting Joy through Gamification

Research reports show that AI, Gamification along with AR/VR are expected to be the biggest growth drivers for the world’s fastest growing edtech market. This backs our strategy to adopt GenAI based features.

Voice Overs : Deliver lessons to students through their favorite personalities, scientists and cartoon characters. 

Interactive Quizzes with Cartoon Characters

Idea2: Personlisation

Individualised Learning Plan and Progress Tracker: One-size doesn’t fit all

Learning plan for each subject/course based on the student’s assessment of current understanding and IQ, Leveling Up, Ancillary Tests, Personalized Feedback:

Collaborative and Community-based learning

Junior Students can also be paired up with other senior students who are interested in mentoring or resolving specific doubts in a subject. This matching can be done using many different aspects in the students profile. This will not only reduce the burden of teachers, but also help in creating a community, enable students to make friends and encourage seniors to teach/mentor their juniors.

Idea3: AI Tools

Idea-3 focuses on using Generative AI to streamline educational content creation and elevate teacher support. This includes automating video creation and grading, providing 1:1 tutoring opportunities, and employing data analytics to enhance personalized teaching and student performance evaluation

Automate video creation to achieve cost-efficiency and free teachers time

Companies like Content Technologies are providing AI technology to break down any textbook into a smart study guide complete with chapter summaries, unlimited true-false and multiple choice practice tests and flashcards all drilled down to a specific textbook, ISBN number, author and chapter.

Streamline the online classes experience

Utilize Generative AI to identify common questions or areas where students struggle on a specific topic. Teachers can prepare these common questions as a part of their curriculum.

Private 1:1 tutoring

The automation by AI can be used to free up teachers time, so that they can be utilized for 1:1 online coaching for students who need 1:1 support. This can also add another revenue stream in addition to the cohort based group classes

Students Performance Analytics Dashboard

Use the exams and assessment data to build performance dashboards which can be used by teachers to manage a kid’s progress. Use AI to analyze student performance data and highlight areas that need improvement, allowing teachers to tailor their instruction more effectively.

Idea4: STEM Education

Language and soft skills

The section advocates for the inclusion of English and communication courses to boost reading and comprehension, key metrics in PISA assessments. It suggests using GenAI and interactive activities like online debates and story writing to make the learning experience engaging and beneficial, especially for students aiming for undergraduate studies abroad.

Coding and Programming Skills

Another report shows that Corporate Learning and Employee Upskilling is an emerging category among developing countries. This can also be viewed as a growth opportunity if the platform focuses on coding and programming skills.

Idea4: World-Class Higher Education

Career Mentorship for 10th-12th Graders

Offer mentorship programs focused on career choices and planning for students in grades 10 through 12. This can also be integrated with coaching and support for students who are interested to pursue under-grad degree abroad

Guidance on Application Process:

More and more Indonesians are traveling abroad to pursue higher education. There is opportunity to add provide consulting services to higher secondary students and help

Expand to GRE/GMAT/SAT Markets

 Target Indonesian students aspiring to study abroad by offering test preparation for exams like GRE, GMAT, and SATs
